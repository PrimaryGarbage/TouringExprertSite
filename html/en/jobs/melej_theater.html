Melezh Theater, Mozyr

2020

In 2020, the long-awaited reconstruction of the drama theater named after Melezh was completed, in the process of which the installation of a sound amplification system, as well as a system of staged lighting in the "large" and "small" halls was carried out.

Sound support in the "Big" Hall is represented by professional systems in the form of a linear array.
8 x JBL VTX A8 and 3 x JBL VTX;
B18 in cardioid configuration;
Monitor Line: 4 x JBL VTX M20;
Portal shots: 4 x JBL SRX 815;
Amplification: Crown I-Tech 4x3500HD;
Digital mixing console: Soundcraft Vi1000.

Staged lighting systems are represented by the brands MARTIN, PROLIGHTS;
Full-turn lighting devices: 16 x Martin ERA 300 Profile; 12 x Martin MAC Aura XB; 24 x Prolights RUBY;
Flood light devices: 8 x Martin RUSH BATTEN 1 HEX;
Profile spotlights of four types: 16 x Martin ELP WW; 12 x Martin ELP CL; 20 x Prolights ECLISEPARDY;
18 x Prolights ECLISEPARFC;
For additional atmospheric effects are used: Martin Jem Compact Haze; Martin Jem ZR35; Antari S-500E;
Remote control for lights and other DMX-compatible devices: ChamSys MagicQ MQ500 .

The "Small" hall

Sound support in the "Small" Hall is represented by professional active JBL SRX815P and SRX818SP systems.
Monitor Line: 4 x JBL SRX812P;
Mixing Console: Soundcraft Signature 12MTK;

Staged lighting systems are represented by PROLIGHTS devices.
Full-turn lighting devices: 8 x ProLights JETSPOT4Z;
Flood Light devices: 22 x Flash LED PAR 64 COB SHORT Mk2 200W RGBW;
Profile spotlights: 12 x Prolights ECLISEPARTY;
For additional atmospheric effects are used: Martin Jem Compact Haze; Martin Jem ZR35;
Light control panel: ChamSys QuickQ 20.
