Audiocenter company

August, 2024

AUDIOCENTER, founded in 2006 in Berlin, has established itself from the very beginning of its activity as a manufacturer of high-end audio equipment that does not copy solutions from other brands. AUDIOCENTER offers speakers and amplifiers with an unsurpassed price/quality ratio, combining advanced technologies from Europe and America with intelligent manufacturing in China.

AUDIOCENTER's motto is "If I was given to see beyond others, it was only because I stood on the shoulders of giants" (Isaac Newton), which emphasizes the company's serious approach to the design and production of its products. Our own research center in Spain, collaboration with leading experts and research institutions around the world - all this allows AUDIOCENTER to create truly outstanding audio solutions.

AUDIOCENTER acoustic-based sound reinforcement systems are successfully used in various projects around the world:

  • Shah Alam and Sarawak Stadiums in Malaysia
  • Television studios in China
  • Numerous festivals and open-air concerts in Germany, Italy, Myanmar, Thailand, and the Philippines

Today, AUDIOCENTER works closely with global Pro Audio market leaders such as Beyma, Pascal, Belden, Neutrik, B&C speakers, Faital PRO, Powersoft, Phoenix contact and others. A striking example of AUDIOCENTER's systematic approach to its products is the Artist line of equipment, which allows you to create sound systems of various formats and purposes: from background dubbing of small cafes to full-fledged systems for large sports arenas.

To promote the brand, AUDIOCENTER has divided the global market into 5 regions. In Europe, AUDIOCENTER has established a branch of TRIUS, which is mainly engaged in research and development, as well as high-level technical support. AUDIOCENTER has established distributors all over the world. In the Republic of Belarus, the distributor is the company "Touring Expert".
